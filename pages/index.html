Joker Hunt

Rules

Joker Hunt is a card game where players draw cards to score points. Avoid drawing a Joker, as it will cost you all your unbanked points! Use picture cards to bank half your points safely. Here's how to play:

  1. Click "Play" to start the game.
  2. On your turn, draw up to three cards.
  3. If you draw a picture card (J, Q, K), you can bank half your points to save them.
  4. If you draw a Joker, you lose all your unbanked points!
  5. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
