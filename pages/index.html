Joker Hunt

Rules

Welcome to Joker Hunt, the exciting card game where luck and strategy combine! Your goal is to outscore your opponents by collecting points while avoiding the mischievous Jokers. Here's a complete guide on how to play:

Setup

  • Decide the number of players (2 to 4).
  • Each player takes turns drawing cards from a shared deck.
  • The game ends when the deck runs out of cards.

Gameplay

  1. Start Your Turn: On your turn, you can draw up to three cards:
    • Click on the deck to draw a card.
    • You can stop drawing at any time, but you can only draw a maximum of three cards per turn.
  2. Scoring:
    • Number cards (2â€“10): Add their value to your live score.
    • Face cards (J, Q, K): Worth 10 points and allow you to bank half your live score.
    • Aces: Worth 11 points.
  3. Joker Effect: If you draw a Joker:
    • You lose all your unbanked (live) points for that turn.
    • Your turn ends immediately, and you must shuffle the deck before the next turn.
  4. Banking Points: You can choose to bank half your live score when you draw a face card (J, Q, K). Banked points are safe and cannot be lost.
  5. Turn End: After drawing up to three cards or choosing to bank points, click "End Turn" to pass the turn to the next player. (Turn will automatically end after 3 cards are drawn)

Winning the Game

  • The game ends when a player reaches 100 points

Strategies and Tips

  • Risk vs. Reward: Decide whether to draw more cards or play it safe and bank points early.
  • Picture Cards: Use them strategically to save points when your live score is high.
  • Avoid Jokers: Be cautious after drawing high-value cards, as a Joker can erase your progress!
  • Keep Track: Pay attention to which cards have already been drawn to improve your decisions.

Good luck and enjoy the thrill of Joker Hunt!
